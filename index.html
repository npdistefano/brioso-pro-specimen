Introduction

BriosoÂ® Pro is a typeface family designed in the calligraphic tradition of our Latin alphabet.
Brioso blends the humanity of written forms with the clarity of digital design, allowing type users to set pages of refined elegance.
Designed by Robert Slimbach, this energetic type family is mocdeled on his own formal roman and italic script.
Named after the Italian word for "lively," words set in Brioso moves rhythmically across the page with an energy that is tempered by an ordered structure and lucid form.
